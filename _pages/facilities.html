仪器介绍

Ultra-low Temperature (300mK) High-magnetic Field (9-2-2T) Scanning Tunneling Microscope

This helium-3 cooled STM system is equipped with a three-dimensional vector magnetic field as well as high-frequency coaxial lines. The high-frequency capability (up to 40GHz with pulsed capabilities) allows for performing ESR on single atoms and molecules on surfaces for quantum sensing of magnetic materials and quantum simulations.

  • 超高真空、极低温（300 mK）、强磁场（9-2-2 T）扫描隧道显微镜
  • 空间精度 0.05 nm、能量分辨率 40 neV

UHV Molecular Beam Epitaxy (MBE) instrument

This UHV MBE system is equipped with several sources for growth of high-quality low-dimensional quantum materials for further study with STM.

  • 超高真空 (10-10 mbar)
  • 各温区蒸发
  • 低温/高温样品台
